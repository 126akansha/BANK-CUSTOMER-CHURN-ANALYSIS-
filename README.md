# 🏦 BANK CUSTOMER CHURN ANALYSIS 
 

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn in the banking sector to understand customer behavior and identify factors affecting customer retention. The analysis helps banks understand why customers leave and which customer segments are more likely to churn. 

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Study the impact of age, balance, salary, products, and tenure.
- Compare active and inactive customers.
- Generate business insights through interactive dashboards.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Active customers are less likely to churn.
- Customer churn varies across different countries.
- Customers with fewer products are more likely to leave the bank.
- Balance and tenure influence customer retention.
- Customer demographics play an important role in churn behavior.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Focus on retaining customers with low tenure.
- Improve engagement with inactive customers.
- Offer personalized products and services.
- Strengthen customer relationship programs.
- Provide special benefits to high-value customers.
- Monitor churn-prone customer segments regularly.
- Increase customer satisfaction through targeted campaigns.

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization techniques can be used to understand customer churn and provide actionable business insights. The combination of Excel, Python, SQL, and Power BI helps transform raw data into meaningful decisions for improving customer retention.
